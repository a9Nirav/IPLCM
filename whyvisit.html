    Why Visit ?

    "PROPEL YOUR BUSINESS FORWARD WITH NEW PRODUCTS FOR YOUR BRAND"

    There’s no better opportunity to explore,

    source and indulge in live demos of Private Label food and non- Food products from around the world for the MENA region. Private Label Middle East is the ONLY event that is dedicated to bringing global manufacturers together to meet with top retailers, e-tailers, and product developers from absolutely any industry that is looking to expand their F&B and FMCG product lines. Be part of MENA’s largest Private Label and contract manufacturing event, taking place from 24-26 September 2024, which will offer products from over 40 countries worldwide and get you connected to the right people for your business.

    "KEY REASONS TO VISIT"

    Gain a years's worth of leads in just three days.

    Meet with various manufacturers of food and non-food products from all over the world under one roof.

    Discover innovation at the heart of the show.

    Immerse yourself in exciting new features that will showcase the industry’s most innovative products on the show floor, taking you on a discovery trail curated to enhance your event experience

    Explore the biggest global representation of private label suppliers in the region.

    Meet over 200 exhibitors, showcasing thousands of products from 40 countries across the globe.

    "MEET THE RIGHT PEOPLE FOR YOUR BUSINESS"

    THE LARGEST MEETING PROGRAMME IS DESIGNED TO CONNECT PRIVATE LABEL BUYERS, MANUFACTURERS AND OWNERS

    Our dedicated business match-making programme for Private Label will ensure you connect with only the people that most matter to your business. Through our app, you will be able to network with manufacturers and visitors prior to the event and schedule face-to-face meetings that ensure you make the most of your time during your visit.
