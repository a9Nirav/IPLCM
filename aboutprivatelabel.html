    About Private Label ME

    Private Label Middle East is MENA’s only event where buyers can get straight to the source for cost-effective customisable products, branding & packaging solutions, bringing together food & and non-food suppliers, private label & contract manufacturers from over 40+ countries across the globe, all conveniently located under one roof.

    Taking place only once a year, the three-day event is endorsed by over 7,500+ visitors from over 88 countries worldwide with some of the biggest names in the region; Amazon, Carrefour, Aldi, Nazih Group, Doubletree by Hilton, Lulu Hypermarkets, Emirates Airlines, Landmark Group, Aster Pharmacy, and many more, making it the only event of its kind for Private Labeling and contract manufacturing.

    The event will see manufacturers on the event floor, exploring opportunities to utilise excess capacity towards more profitable lines. Whilst, brand owners, category managers, procurement heads, distributors, traders, buyers, chefs, and other product developers discover thousands of products to win customers with their own unique brand offerings.
