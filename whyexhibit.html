    "EXHIBITOR OPPORTUNITIES"

    The best option for unlimited flexibility, customisation and maximum impact, you can appoint a stand builder and create a stand to meet your requirements and budget. The minimum size for Space Only is 9 sqm.

    EXHIBITION SPACE PRICE INCLUDES:

    • Floor space only, ready for you to design and build your own custom stand
    • Inclusion in the Private Label Middle East exhibitor list, the mobile app and other promotional materials
    • A basic listing in the show E-Zone with the opportunity to upgrade
    • Access to a wide range of sponsorship (additional cost), promotional and event opportunities throughout the year and during show

    With a shell scheme you are getting a pre-built space that requires minimal customization. This will be perfect if you need to turn-up-and-show in the shortest time possible without any hassle. The package provides a shell – walls, carpet, spotlights and power socket – creating the perfect base for you to add as necessary. The minimum size for a shell scheme is (4m x3m)12sqm and all stand walls are 3m high..

    SHELL SCHEME PACKAGE STAND RATE INCLUDES:

    • Syma X-Wall structure shell scheme walls (3m high)
    • Company name (Text only) and stand number on the fascia panel
    • Exhibition grade Carpet
    • LED spotlights – 3nos x 20 Watts SYMA LED spotlights (per 9sqm)
    • UK 3Pin wall Socket of 200Watts/220V (1x socket per every 9sqm)
    • Inclusion in the Private Label Middle East exhibitor list, the mobile app and other promotional materials
    • A basic listing in the show E-Zone with the opportunity to upgrade
    • Access to a wide range of sponsorship (additional cost), promotional and event opportunities throughout the year and during show
    • Access to the business matchmaking program
    • Opportunity to submit editorial and press releases

    Available in various sizes, from large theatres to small rooms, DWTC Meeting Rooms are available daily to companies with main exhibition stands. Ideal for meetings, press events or demos.

    These rooms are found off the show floor in areas around the entire DWTC venue, accessible by foot, elevator or staircase.
